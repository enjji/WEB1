The World Wide Web, the WWW or the Web refers to an information space where documents and other web resources are identified by Uniform Resource Locators (URLs), interlinked by hypertext links, and and accessible via the Internet. [1] English scientist Tim Berners-Lee invented teh World Wibe Web in 1989. He wrote the first web browser in 1990 while employed at CERN in switzerland.[2][3] The browser was released outside CERN in 1991, first to other research instituions starting in January 1991 and to the general public on the Internet in August 1991.
